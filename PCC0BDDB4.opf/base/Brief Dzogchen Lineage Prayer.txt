Brief Prayer to the Dzogchen Lineage
by Mipham Rinpoche

kunzang garab shri seng pema jung
Samantabhadra, Garab Dorje, Śrī Siṃha, Padmākara,
longchen rabjam tsawé lama sok
Longchen Rabjam, my own root Guru and the rest—
gong da nyengyü lamar solwa deb
Masters of mind, sign and oral lineages of transmission, to you I pray!
dzogchen gongpa tokpar jingyi lob
Grant your blessing so that I may realize the mind of Great Perfection.

Mipham Ösel Dorje wrote this at the request of devoted Ösel. Virtue!
